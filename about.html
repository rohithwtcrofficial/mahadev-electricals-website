Welcome to Mahadev Electricals

At Mahadev Electricals, we are committed to providing the highest quality electrical supplies, PVC pipes, tiles, sanitary wares, doors, and paints to our valued customers in Singanallur and throughout Coimbatore.

As an authorized dealer for premium brands like V-Guard, Havells, Fybros, Kundan, Lisha, GM, and Legrand, we ensure that every product we offer meets the highest standards of quality and reliability.

Our experienced team is dedicated to helping you find the perfect solutions for your electrical and construction needs, whether you're a homeowner, contractor, or business owner.
