FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything You Need to Know About Our Services

Do you offer delivery across Coimbatore?

Yes! We provide fast and reliable delivery across Singanallur, Coimbatore and surrounding areas. For large bulk orders, we coordinate site delivery directly. Call us at 9363387963 to schedule your delivery.

Are all your products genuine and authorized?

Absolutely. Mahadev Electricals is an authorized dealer for V-Guard, Havells, Fybros, Kundan, Lisha, GM, and Legrand. Every product we sell comes directly from the brand's official distribution channel with proper warranty documentation.

Do you provide GST invoices for bulk purchases?

Yes, we are a GST registered business. Proper tax invoices are provided for all purchases, making it easy for contractors, builders, and businesses to maintain accounts and claim input credits.

Can I get a quote for my construction project?

Definitely! Share your requirements via WhatsApp or call us, and our expert team will prepare a detailed quote covering all materials — electrical wires, pipes, tiles, sanitary, paints and more. We support both small homes and large commercial projects.

What are your business hours?

We are open Monday to Saturday from 9:00 AM to 8:00 PM and Sundays from 9:00 AM to 2:00 PM. You can also reach us via WhatsApp anytime for enquiries at 9363387963.
